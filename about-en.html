About SECA

A trusted environmental consulting firm specializing in air quality assessment and atmospheric dispersion modeling since 1995.

Our Company

SECA - Environmental and Climatic Studies System Ltd. is a company specialized in providing consulting services, studies and projects that has been operating in the environmental area with an emphasis on air pollution since 1995.

Based on the experience of its technical staff, SECA has been executing services and advising companies with updated technology while maintaining the commitment to quality and impartiality.

Our company uses specialized mathematical models for the evaluation of the dispersion of atmospheric pollutants, with validated and internationally recognized methods. We apply these models to evaluate existing situations or future scenarios, in studies required for the environmental licensing of industrial complexes or for the evaluation of specific air quality situations.
