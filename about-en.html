The Company

SECA - Environmental and Climatic Studies System Ltd. is a company specialized in providing consulting services, studies and projects that has been operating in the environmental area with an emphasis on air pollution since 1995. Based on the experience of its technical staff, SECA has been executing services and advising companies with updated technology while maintaining the commitment to quality and impartiality.

Technical Team

It is constituted by professionals with great experience and competence, with training acquired in Brazil and other countries. In addition to theoretical training, they have practical knowledge for carrying out projects and studies, acquired for years in private companies and public bodies in the environmental area.

SECA's technical team is led by SILVIO DE OLIVEIRA.
