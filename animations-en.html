Professional Experience in Environmental Visualization

SECA has over 20 years of experience in creating advanced animations and visualizations for environmental studies, with a focus on:

  • Mathematical modeling of atmospheric dispersion
  • Air quality control visualization
  • Climate and meteorological pattern analysis
  • Environmental risk assessment
  • Public consultation and stakeholder presentations

Our animations are created using state-of-the-art tools and are based on rigorous scientific modeling, making complex environmental processes easier to understand for both technical and non-technical audiences.

Applications

How our animations are used in environmental management

Environmental Impact Assessments

Our animations are used in environmental impact studies to visualize potential impacts of proposed projects, helping stakeholders understand complex environmental consequences.

Public Consultations

Visual representations help communicate environmental data to the public during consultations for new industrial projects or environmental management plans.

Educational Resources

Our animations serve as valuable educational tools for universities, training programs, and public awareness campaigns about environmental processes.

Industrial Planning

Companies use our visualizations to optimize industrial layouts, stack heights, and operating conditions to minimize environmental impacts.

Expert Testimony

Our animations help explain complex environmental phenomena in legal proceedings, regulatory hearings, and expert testimony situations.

Results Presentation

Converting technical modeling results into visual formats makes them more accessible and impactful for executive presentations and reports.
