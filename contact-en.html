Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly.

Do you offer services internationally?

Yes, SECA provides services throughout South America, North America, and Europe. We have conducted projects in over 25 countries and maintain international offices to support our global clients.

Can you provide references for similar projects?

Absolutely. We're happy to provide relevant references and case studies for projects similar to your needs. Please specify your industry and project type when contacting us, and we'll share appropriate references.

How can I schedule a consultation with your experts?

You can request a consultation by filling out the contact form on this page, specifying that you'd like to schedule a meeting. Our team will follow up to arrange a time that works for both parties, either in person or via video conference.
