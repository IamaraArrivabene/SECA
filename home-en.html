Featured Project

Air Quality Assessment for Industrial Complex

SECA conducted a comprehensive air quality assessment for a major industrial complex, involving dispersion modeling, monitoring station setup, and regulatory compliance analysis.

  • Installation of state-of-the-art meteorological stations for accurate data collection
  • Multi-year data analysis using advanced statistical methods
  • Sophisticated mathematical modeling using AERMOD, CALPUFF, and proprietary SECA methodologies

Results enabled the client to demonstrate regulatory compliance, optimize operations, and implement cost-effective emissions control strategies.
