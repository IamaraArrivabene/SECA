SECA - Environmental and Climatic Studies System Ltd. is a company specialized in providing consulting services, studies and projects that has been operating in the environmental area with an emphasis on air pollution since 1995. Based on the experience of its technical staff, it has been executing services and advising companies with updated technology and with this while maintaining the commitment to quality and impartiality.
