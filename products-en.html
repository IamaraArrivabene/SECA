Software Solutions

SECA is an official distributor in Brazil of Lakes Environmental Software, a reseller of Lakes products in the area of software for modeling atmospheric dispersion.

AERMOD View™

A complete and powerful air dispersion modeling package that incorporates the latest U.S. EPA air dispersion models:

  • AERMOD (AMS/EPA Regulatory Model)
  • ISCST3 (Industrial Source Complex)
  • ISC-PRIME

Features include:

  • PRIME building downwash algorithms
  • Advanced depositional parameters
  • Local terrain effects
  • Advanced meteorological turbulence calculations

CALPUFF View™

A complete graphical solution for the CALPUFF modeling system, including CALPUFF, CALMET, CALPOST, and related pre- and post-processors.

Available versions:

  • USEPA Approved Version 5.8
  • Version 6
  • Version 7

Applications include:

  • Toxic pollutant deposition
  • Visibility assessments
  • Complex terrain modeling
  • Secondary pollutant formation
  • Long range and overwater transport
  • Building downwash effects

Weather Stations

Practical, versatile and economical monitoring solutions. We provide station supply, installation, training, data processing and analysis for environmental studies and various applications.

Station Components

  • Relative humidity and air temperature sensor
  • Anemometer - Wind speed and direction measurement
  • Pluviometer - Rainfall measurement
  • Leaf wetness sensor
  • UV sensor - Solar, global and ultraviolet radiation measurement
  • System failure alarm
  • Data logger with real-time preview
  • Actinograph - Solar radiation intensity measurement and recording
