Professional Training

SECA offers specialized training in mathematical modeling of atmospheric dispersion of pollutants, with emphasis on the use of AERMOD and CALPUFF models. Our training programs are designed for professionals from:

  • Environmental consulting companies
  • Industries
  • Environmental agencies
  • Universities and research institutions

Our courses combine theoretical foundations with extensive hands-on practice, ensuring participants gain practical experience with the tools and methodologies used in environmental impact assessment.

Training Programs

We offer comprehensive training in atmospheric dispersion modeling and environmental monitoring

AERMOD Training

Comprehensive training on the AERMOD modeling system, including:

  • AERMOD model structure and algorithms
  • AERMET meteorological data processing
  • AERMAP terrain processing
  • Building downwash with BPIP-PRIME
  • Source characterization
  • Model evaluation
  • Regulatory applications

CALPUFF Training

In-depth training on the CALPUFF modeling system, covering:

  • CALPUFF model capabilities
  • CALMET meteorological modeling
  • Geophysical data preprocessing
  • Long-range transport
  • Complex terrain applications
  • Chemical transformation
  • Postprocessing with CALPOST
