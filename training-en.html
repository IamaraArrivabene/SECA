SECA provides training in the area of operational use of atmospheric dispersion models for air pollution; physical fundamentals of the atmosphere for air pollution control; and characterization of air quality and climate. The courses can be presented in company, online for governmental and non-governmental organizations, public and private companies, consulting companies, universities and associations related to the environment.

Course Topics

- Physical Fundamentals of the Atmosphere for Air Pollution Control;

- Atmospheric Dispersion Modeling for Industrial District Planning, Industrial Installation and Expansion of New Developments;

- Processing of meteorological data and air quality for EIA/RIMA;
